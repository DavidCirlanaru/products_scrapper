The governors and I would like to invite you to take part in our stakeholder consultation for the school potentially becoming an Academy.

The information on these pages explains what an Academy is, how it differs from a typically maintained school and why a school might wish to become an academy.

We welcome the contributions of stakeholders to the process. We hope you are able to read the documents to find out more information and attend meetings on the dates given.

You can also email for answers to any queries you might have @ consultation@queensmill.lbhf.sch.uk
